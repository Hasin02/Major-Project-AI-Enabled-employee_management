Introduction

An Employee Management System (EMS) is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline and optimize various aspects of workforce management within an organization. It typically encompasses functionalities such as attendance tracking, payroll processing, performance evaluation, and employee scheduling. EMS platforms not only facilitate administrative tasks but also enhance communication between management and staff through features like self-service portals and messaging systems.
